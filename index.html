Welcome!

This is a professional pizzeria studio by the name of Project Pizza. It is here where we create individual masterpieces, slow cook these works of art in a coal-fire oven right in front of our patrons, and then serve them on a platter as they were meant to be served - with a knife and fork. We offer many different types of beverages to those so inclined, but there are no sides here. Everything - from the moment you walk through the doors, to the moment you leave - is pizza, pizza, pizza.

Curious? Good.

Pizza is one of the greatest foods ever created by man. It's a fusion of elements so bare and rustic, so truly incorruptible and renowned, that any person off the streets could mimic its design. However, we've got a lot more in store for you today than just pizza.
